# ChordPotion Mapping Rules

- Strong movement maps to `strong_movement` intent with medium density and voice-leading bias.
- Low clutter maps to sparse rhythms, fewer simultaneous notes, and longer gates where appropriate.
- Voicing targets stay within 2-5 notes per event to avoid overcrowding.
- Lead-protective textures reduce upper-register overlap and keep chord backing supportive.
- Rhythmic textures map to controlled syncopation plus light/medium humanization.
- Ambient textures prefer wide voicings with sparse note probability.
- Default BPM is 100 unless `chordpotion_default_bpm` is configured locally.
- Mapping is local-only and never implies plugin availability without local config or scanner confirmation.
